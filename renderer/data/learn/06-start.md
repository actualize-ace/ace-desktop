# Starting your day

`/start` is the morning anchor. Not a task review. Not a productivity warm-up. A grounding ritual that puts you in your seat before the day asks anything of you.

When you run it, ACE reads your state file, your active outcomes, and your calendar. Then it walks the three legs of the Triad with you, in order.

**Authority.** Who is authoring today? The identity line lands first. One grounding question follows. No rush.

**Capacity.** What can the body actually hold? A somatic check-in, an honest energy read, any regulation you need before execution.

**Expansion.** What gets built today? Priority stack, calendar, follow-ups, one clear needle-mover.

The sequence matters. Authority before capacity. Capacity before execution. Skip a leg and the day fragments — you'll feel it by 2pm.

Done well, `/start` takes five minutes and saves you hours of drift. You leave it knowing what you're authoring, what your body can carry, and the one thing that moves the needle.
