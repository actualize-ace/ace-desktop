# The Command Center

The Home view is your Command Center. Not a dashboard of metrics — a live mirror of where you actually are.

Three things hold the surface together.

**Your Triad deck.** Authority, Capacity, Expansion — reading your current state across all three legs. When one goes quiet or red, you see it before it breaks something downstream. This is how the system tells you what needs attention before you have to ask.

**Your North Star.** The single Definite Chief Aim this season of your life is serving. Everything the system does — every widget, every nudge, every ritual — is in service of that aim. If the North Star is wrong, nothing else lands. If it's right, the whole surface clarifies.

**Your widgets.** Real outcomes, pipeline, follow-ups, signals — populated from files inside your vault. Not a template you fill in. A reflection of work already in motion. When you update the underlying file, the widget updates. One source of truth, many views.

No charts for the sake of charts. Nothing that flatters. Just the state of the system, read back to you honestly.

Open Home and look around. See what your system is actually tracking.

*The mirror only works when you let it show you what's there.*
