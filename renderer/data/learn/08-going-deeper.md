# Going deeper

The rails are in your hands. Vault, Command Center, chat, session rhythm, `/start`, `/eod`. That's the daily spine.

Everything else in ACE is optional surface — there when you want it, quiet when you don't. The Deeper track in this Learn tab covers:

- **Insight** — voice coaching with live transcription
- **Breath** — guided protocols for regulation
- **Oracle** — pattern reflection from your own history
- **Astro** — your natal blueprint as a read
- **Artifacts** — creative work, catalogued
- **People** — your network as living context
- **Graph** — the shape of your vault, visually
- **Agent Terminal** — direct CLI, no chat wrapper
- **Lean mode** — minimum-footprint working surface
- **Settings** — themes, zoom, guardrails, MCP

Browse them whenever you want. Learn stays in the sidebar.
