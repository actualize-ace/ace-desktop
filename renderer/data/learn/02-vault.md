# Your vault, your sovereignty

Everything in ACE is a markdown file on your machine. Your daily notes, your decisions, your people, your patterns — all plain text, sitting in folders you can open in any editor, back up, or carry anywhere.

This isn't a technical detail. It's the whole design.

**Portable.** Your life's context is never locked inside a SaaS silo. Export is not a feature here — it's the default state.

**Inspectable.** Nothing is hidden in a database you can't see. If ACE wrote it, you can read it, edit it, or delete it.

**No lock-in.** If you ever stop using this app, your vault keeps working. Obsidian, VS Code, plain Finder — take your pick.

**AI reads from you, not about you.** ACE treats your files as the source of truth. The intelligence layer serves the files. You own them.

Open the Vault tab and look around. These are your words, in your folders, on your disk.

*Sovereignty isn't a claim. It's the file on your machine.*
