0 — Orb: Resting. Single merged orb, breathing glow. System idle.
1 — Triad: Awakening. Orb splits into Authority + Capacity + Expansion.
2 — Triangle: Emerging. Curvilinear void appears and grows. Streaming begins.
3 — Engine: Running. Triangle spins clockwise, accelerating. Full power.
↓ Wind Down: Graceful decay. Spin decelerates. Triangle fades. Circles re-emerge and merge. Glow contracts last.
